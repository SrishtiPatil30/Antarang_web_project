ABOUT ANTARANG

Vision

Blind and Partially Sighted youth of Belgavi must enjoy a dignified life in an inclusive society.

Mission

To build the capacity of blind and partially sighted youth so that they are empowered to claim and utilize their rights.

Goal

To improve the living standards of blind and partially sighted youth by increasing access to blind-friendly education, health and employment services.

Objectives

  • First Music school of Belagavi run by the blind teacher.
  • Antarng is first blind orchestra of behavior.
  • Create stage for blind friends to support in their mobility, education, sports, music, computer training,etc
  • To carry out advocacy on our own, or through networking with others to secure the rights of blind and partially sighted people.
  • To increase access to education for blind and partially sighted people.
  • To promote the employment and self-employment of the blind and partially sighted.
